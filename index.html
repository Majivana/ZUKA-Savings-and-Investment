The Challenge Facing Our Youth

Historical barriers continue to limit opportunities for young people to build financial security and contribute meaningfully to economic development.

Breaking Down the Barriers

Historical student debt, skills gaps, and lack of financial literacy continue to undermine the progress and growth of young people, preventing them from becoming positive contributors in their own socio-economic development and that of their country.

These systemic challenges create a cycle that's difficult to break without targeted intervention and accessible financial instruments designed specifically for youth needs.

Discover Our Solution

Student Debt Burden

Overwhelming debt prevents graduates from investing in their future and building wealth early. This creates a significant barrier to financial independence and long-term prosperity.

Skills Gap

Lack of practical financial knowledge creates barriers to making sound investment decisions. Many young people simply don't have the education needed to navigate complex financial systems.

Limited Access

Traditional financial systems often exclude youth and unemployed graduates from wealth-building opportunities. High minimum investments and complex requirements create unnecessary barriers.

Economic Exclusion

Many young people are locked out of the formal economy due to lack of credit history, collateral, or established income streams. This perpetuates cycles of poverty and limits economic mobility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with ZUKA Savings and Investment.

How much do I need to start investing?

You can start with as little as R50 per month. We believe that everyone should have access to wealth-building tools regardless of their current financial situation. Our flexible contribution options allow you to invest what you can afford and increase as your income grows.

Is my money safe with ZUKA?

Absolutely. ZUKA is fully licensed and regulated by the Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA). All funds are held in segregated accounts with top-tier banking partners, and our investment strategies focus on balanced portfolios with appropriate risk management. Your financial security is our highest priority.

How does ZUKA differ from traditional banks?

Unlike traditional banks that focus on products and fees, ZUKA is built around your financial journey. We combine investment opportunities with comprehensive education, mentorship, and community support. Our fee structure is transparent and significantly lower than traditional investment platforms, allowing more of your money to work for you.

Can I access my money when I need it?

Yes, you have flexible access to your funds. While we encourage long-term investing for maximum growth, you can withdraw your contributions at any time. Some investment options may have specific terms, but we always clearly communicate these before you invest, ensuring you can make informed decisions that match your financial goals and timeline.
